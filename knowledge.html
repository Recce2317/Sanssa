General

How To Enter

The names of all learners wishing to enter, must be listed on the "Official SKE Entry Form". These are obtainable by completing the attached "Request for Official Entry Forms" tear-off slip.


Please request these NOW and return completed Official Entry Forms with the required Entry Fees to reach SANSSA Regional Office by :


21st May


Instructions and question papers will be mailed to each entrant prior to the examination date.


LATE ENTRIES CANNOT BE ACCEPTED!


LANGUAGE MEDIUM


Question papers are available in all South Africa's official languages, depending on the availability of translators.


ENTRY FEES

Member Church

R20 per pupil | R30,00 per teacher

Non Member Church

R20 per pupil | R30,00 per teacher

EXAMINATION DATE


June of Every Year


RESULTS

Results will be available by November, whereupon you will be notified by post.


AWARDS


Certificates are awarded to all learners achieving 40% or more. learners who achieve 80-90% will receive a First Class Certificate. Learners who achieve 90-100% will receive a Distinction Certificate.
